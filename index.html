Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba

Tanjiro Kamado

Tanjiro Kamado is the main character of the “Demon Slayer” anime series. He is the first-born son of the Kamado family whose life turned into a nightmare after he returned from his trade of selling charcoal to discover that his family had been brutally murdered by the demon ruler Muzan Kibutsuji.

Zenitsu Agatsuma

Zenitsu Agatsuma is one of the six main characters in the anime series and a member of the Demon Slayer Corps who uses the Thunder Breathing technique. He is a close friend of Tanjiro, Nezuko, and Inosuke and is considered a coward despite his powerful abilities. Though he was forced to join the Demon Slayers Corps to pay his debt, his cowardly personality hinders him from utilizing his abilities while in a conscious state. Over the course of the series, he learns to overcome his fear, and he matures to the point of developing a new thunder breathing technique.

Inosuke Hashibira

Inosuke Hashibira is one of Kimetsu no Yaiba’s main characters in Demon Slayers. He is a member of the Demon Slayer Corps who uses a self-taught Beast Breathing along with two serrated swords in battle. He is notably known for the hollowed-out bear head he wears as a mask in honor of his mother, Katoha Hashibira, who was murdered by the upper Moon Two demon, Doma of the Twelve Kizuki.

Genya Shinazugawa

Genya Shinazugawa is one of the main characters of Demon Slayers. He is a Demon Slayer and the younger brother of Sanemi Shinazugawa, the wind Hashira. Like Tanjiro and Inosuke, he also has a tragic story as he witnessed his demonized mother murder his siblings.

Akaza

Akaza is Demon Slayer’s major supporting antagonist and the best-written character in the series. He introduced himself in the Demon Slayer: Mugen Train Movie as a demon holding the third highest rank, Upper-rank Three with the Twelve Kizuki. His backstory has made him a more pitiful character than even the main character.

Aoba Hashibira

Aoba Hashibira is a well-known botanist involved in researching the Blue Spider Lily. He is the great-grandson of Inosuke Hashibira, Aoi Kanzaki, and Kotoha Hashibira, and shares a striking resemblance with Inosuke, with both having feminine features. Despite the similarities in appearance, Aoba is presented as a timid and brilliant personality, in contrast to Inosuke’s haughtiness.

Demon Slayer

Demon Slayer has been one of the most popular anime series since its TV premiere in 2019. This is not surprising considering the fantastic plot story, suspense, intense fight scenes, stunning visuals, and the plethora of demon slayer characters who have dazzled fans with their fascinating power and interesting personalities.
